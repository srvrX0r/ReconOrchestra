## Reporting a Vulnerability


If you believe you have found a security issue in this project, please:
1. Send an email to rc9000x3d@protonmail.me stating your proof of authorization and the affected artifact.
2. Use the responsible-disclosure template at docs/disclosure_template.md.


We will respond within 7 business days.
